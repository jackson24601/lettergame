Ready to play?

Use the arrow buttons or keyboard to guide falling letters. Match uppercase with lowercase to make a splash.

How to play

  • Move letters left and right as they fall.
  • Drop an uppercase letter onto its lowercase match, or the other way around.
  • Every correct pair gives 1 point and clears both blocks.
  • New letters and faster drops arrive as your score grows.

Keyboard: arrows to move, space to drop, P to pause.
